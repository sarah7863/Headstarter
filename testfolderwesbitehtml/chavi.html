Chavi

Chavi is there to keep your belongings safe while you take a well needed study break.

Ideation

The objective of this project was to become a ”fly on the wall” in a specific environment at Carleton University and identify an opportunity for innovation.

After spending some time at the MacOdrum Library on campus, I recognized that many students studied by themselves and were unable to step away - not even for a moment, With this opportunity, I designed Chavi.

With a convenient desk pad surface to work on, pull down cover and key card activated lock/unlock mechanism. Chavi is designed to provide temporary security to keep students mind at ease when they step away from their belongings. In addition, Chavi is a tabletop solution that can be added on any existing table/desk surface.
